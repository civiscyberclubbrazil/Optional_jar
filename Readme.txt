Put into this plugin folder optional libs with restricted distribution licenses.
They will be dynamically loaded at runtime.

Examples:
- java-dbx-1.1-p3.jar: to decode Outlook Express DBX email boxes
- jai-imageio-jpeg2000-1.3.0.jar: to decode jpeg2000 images in PDFs (https://github.com/jai-imageio/jai-imageio-jpeg2000)
- oracle-jdbc: to connect to Oracle databases (http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/database/features/jdbc/index-091264.html)
